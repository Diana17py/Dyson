Dyson V9 digital motor

Featuring a digitally controller motor, the Dyson Supersonic hair dryer delivers powerful, focused airflow to dry and style hair quickly.Powerful air flow allows you to dry hair not only due to high temperatures.

Air MultiplierTM technology

Due to the fact that the motor is located in the handle, it is possible to integrate the patented Air MultiplierTM technology into the device, which increases the air flow power by 3 times.
